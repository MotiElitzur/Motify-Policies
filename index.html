By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

A Clear Note on Proximity Data

Our service connects you with nearby users by using your device's Bluetooth signal. We want to be clear:

  • We do not use or request GPS location data. Our functionality is based on short-range Bluetooth proximity only.
  • To be compliant with app store policies, we declare the collection of "Approximate Location." This is because using Bluetooth to detect nearby devices is categorized as a form of approximate location by platform operators. Our use is strictly for discovering other users in your immediate vicinity, not for tracking your physical location history.

10. Account & Data Deletion Instructions

You have the right to delete your Motify account and its associated data at any time. This page provides clear instructions on how to do so.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Motify app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Profile screen by tapping the profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Tap the "Delete Account" button.
  4. A confirmation prompt will appear. Confirm that you wish to permanently delete your account.

Once confirmed, we will begin the process of permanently deleting your account and associated personal data from our live systems. Residual data may remain in secure backups for a limited time for disaster recovery before being permanently erased.
